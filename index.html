Boycott Israeli Products

A Peaceful Non Violent Protest

A call for a global boycott against Israeli companies, and businesses operating substantially in Israel, aims to encourage put pressure on the Israeli gouvernment to stop settlements and comply with international law. This economic pressure is intended to promote dialogue and actions that mitigate ongoing suffering and advance a resolution to the conflict. Mindful consumption choices can empower individuals globally to support this peaceful endeavor towards a harmonized region.

  • Understand the goals of the boycott and the reasons behind it.

  • Boycott the Brands that provide services or infrastructure to the Israeli military or settlements.

  • Avoid hate speech or targeting individuals.

  • Hand out flyers or put up posters in your community ensure you're compliant with local laws.

  • Join or start campaigns to ask retailers to stop stocking certain products.
